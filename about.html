About Gera Enterprises

Gera Enterprises is a trusted name in the trading of Ferrous and Non-Ferrous iron and steel scrap for over 46 years. Established in 1979, we have built a reputation for reliability, quality, and professionalism in the Indian and international markets.

We deal in HMS scrap, cast iron, copper, MS plates, TMT cutting, motor scrap, alloy steel and many more categories that meet industrial reprocessing and manufacturing needs. Our experience ensures timely delivery and transparent business practices.
