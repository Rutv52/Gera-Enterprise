About Us

Gera Enterprises is a trusted name in the Iron and Steel trading industry, proudly established in 1979. With over 46 years of experience, we have built a solid reputation for quality, commitment, and reliability.

We specialize in Ferrous and Non-Ferrous metal trading, including materials like HMS Scrap, MS Plates, TMT Cutting, Cast Iron, Alloy Steel, and more. Our professional approach, long-standing industry relationships, and timely delivery have helped us serve clients both in India and internationally.

We are based in India and deal internationally with clients in multiple sectors, delivering reliable solutions in scrap and structural materials. Our goal is to maintain long-term partnerships through trust and transparency.
