About Us

Gera Enterprises is a trusted name in the Iron and Steel trading industry, proudly established in 1979. With over 46 years of experience, we have built a solid reputation for quality, commitment, and reliability.

We specialize in Ferrous and Non-Ferrous metal trading, including materials like HMS Scrap, MS Plates, TMT Cutting, Cast Iron, Alloy Steel, and more. Our professional approach, long-standing industry relationships, and timely delivery have helped us serve hundreds of satisfied clients.

Based in India, Gera Enterprises proudly deals with both national and international clients. We are committed to delivering high-quality materials, transparent pricing, and long-term partnerships in the iron and steel trading world.

Whether you're looking for premium-grade scrap materials or structured supply for industrial needs, Gera Enterprises is your reliable partner.
