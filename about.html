About Us

Established Since 1979 – 46 Years of Trust.

At Gera Enterprises, we take pride in being a trusted name in the iron and steel scrap trading industry. With nearly five decades of expertise, we specialize in sourcing, processing, and delivering both ferrous and non-ferrous metals across India and globally.

Over the years, our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has helped us become a preferred partner for industries, factories, and recycling units. Whether it's TMT Cuttings, HMS Scrap, MS Plates, Motor Scrap, or Alloy Steel, our wide range of products reflects our dedication to excellence.

With a highly experienced team and modern infrastructure, Gera Enterprises continues to grow while staying rooted in its legacy of ethics, transparency, and customer-first values.

We are based in India but serve clients internationally — delivering not just scrap, but trust and quality that last a lifetime.
